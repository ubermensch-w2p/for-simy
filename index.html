A Mixed Heart

I must start with the honest admission that I was pretty optimistic that we'd be having a good time on our trip. After my 2nd trip to Bangalore, we were talking like crazy and had developed all the comfort needed for us to go on a trip together. I knew we could talk for hours at a time without noticing the clock. But at times I wondered if 9 days would be just too much even for us to talk and talk and talk. Isiliye sometimes I feared that we may just have planned the trip a bit too long. Then it was also going to be my first trip ever with any girl, so I was nervous on that part too. So to start off, I was having mixed feelings about the trip. But even so, I promised myself not to overthink and to approach the trip with an open heart..

Love, Not Lust
